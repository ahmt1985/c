While providing high download speed, rich functionality and smooth experience, EagleGet insists on freeware license without any additional conditions. No feature or time limitations, no spyware or malware. Since the content itself is free, you of course do not need to pay to download it.

With EagleGet’s clean and tidy interface, you can add and manage download tasks easily. The navigation panel on the left allows you to easily filter out the needed files. To download online videos, just hover your mouse cursor over the video player and then click “Download” button, the video will be downloaded directly to your hard disk. No matter which browser you are using, you can always enjoy the maximum download speed without boundaries.

After the download is complete, EagleGet will display a pop-up window as well as play a sound to notify you. You can open the downloaded file immediately, or jump to the corresponding folder.

Click the configuration button on the top of main window, you can specify the download directory, resident behavior, as well as toggle other settings, such as clipboard monitoring, browser integration and more.
